What We Do

We get involved in the basics

Be it electrical fixtures or data points, AC ducts or concealed plumbing, we believe that customization starts here and we get started on your home as early as construction drawings get finalised. We take you through the construction, installations and space design stages. Your dedicated lifestylist will constantly stay in touch with you and keep you updated with the details on space planning, material finishes, 3D views fabrications, time plans, budgets and more through a planned portfolio. From detailed research and optimised sourcing, to ordering materials and regular follow ups with the contractors, we are a hands on team that manages it all for you. You will have complete access to us through the entire duration of the project.

Quality Assurance

We aim at delivering services by utilizing advanced technologies and exceeding customers expectations in terms of quality, safety and timely completion. We plan and execute all the activities as per the Project specific quality plan, method instructions and formats as required by the contractual agreement. Besides being art and aesthetic fanatics, we are sticklers for quality, which is why we partner only with industry leaders.
