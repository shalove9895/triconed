Our Projects

At Tricon, great interior design is our business and our passion. We believe that a house becomes a home when it’s personalized — your space should show off your tastes and personality, work with your lifestyle, and maybe make your guests a little jealous. We know that creating a home interior that does all of these things isn’t easy, and we’re here to help. From discovering your personal style to knocking down that wall that’s been preventing the open-concept living area you’ve been lusting after, we’ll make sure that your space isn’t just aesthetically pleasing, but also a reflection of you.
