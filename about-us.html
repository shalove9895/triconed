About Us

About Us

Tricon Interiors LLC is the customized interior design solution for residential and commercial projects. We design spaces that echo your style. These unique interior spaces are designed to match your personality and suit your lifestyle. We know that creating an interior space that does all of these things isn't easy, and so we are here to help. Our dedicated life stylist works together to provide you with details about space planning, materials finishes, 3D visuals, time plans, budgets, execution services and much more through an outlined portfolio.

Whatever it is you desire, we can help you source out everything to suit your preferences and comfort. Be it soft upholstery, wall finishes, flooring, furniture fit outs or accessories we guide you all the way through to the final installations to create your desired space.

Our Vision
Tricon aims to be market leaders and the most preferred customized interior designing firm in UAE. Our vision is to provide social impact solutions, be responsive and work together towards a sustainable green future for the benefit of all.

Your Unique Style. Your Unique Space!
From a cushy reading corner to a noisy kid’s room or an office workstation to a soundproof conference room, we can custom style all of it. Minimalist or the lavishly opulent, our lifestylists work closely with you to understand your style. Every single element from floor finishes to ceiling fixtures, wall textures to wood grains, built in furniture to soft furnishings and accessories every single element is cherry picked to your ultimate liking.

We strike a balance between form and function to create your desired living space.

Your space is a reflection of who you are and that’s why, at Tricon Interiors L.L.C., we design spaces that echo your style. Whatever it is you desire, we custom make everything to suit your preferences & comfort; from space planning, furniture fit outs & selections of accessories all the way through to the final installations.
